About Us

FT FINANCIAL LLC is a finance, audit, and accounting consulting services firm specializing in providing financial planning, tax planning and compliance, financial analysis, business valuation, cash flow management and debt management advisory and accounting and bookkeeping (including tax filing and preparation) services. We are headquartered in North Carolina, with a dedicated team of professionals with extensive experience in the field.

Our mission is to help businesses in the United States, particularly small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), reach their financial goals by providing them with the guidance, expertise, and support they need to succeed. We are committed to building long-term relationships with our clients and providing them with personalized service that is tailored to their individual needs.

We believe in the power of transparency and communication, and we are dedicated to keeping our clients informed every step of the way. Our commitment to excellence is evident in the quality of our work, and we are always striving to improve our services and stay up-to-date on the latest industry best practices.

At FT FINANCIAL LLC, we are passionate about helping businesses succeed and we are confident that we have the experience, skills, and dedication to help our clients achieve their financial goals. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help your business thrive.

About Me

Fellippe Camara Taborelli da Silva is a seasoned accounting professional with a successful career that crosses borders. His experience encompasses both Brazil, his homeland, and the United States, where he seeks to build a new story.

In Brazil, Fellippe built a solid path in accounting, working in various positions that allowed him to develop comprehensive skills and knowledge. His journey began at JDrel Sistemas de Informática Ltda, where he worked as a Support Analyst, providing technical support for the Domínio system by Thomson Reuters. This experience allowed him to hone his skills in problem-solving, customer service, and training, while expanding his knowledge of SPED Fiscal, SPED ECF, and eSocial systems, essential for Brazilian tax compliance.

Next, Fellippe took on the role of Accounting Manager at Interativa Contabilidade, where he led and supervised the daily operations of the accounting department. During his tenure, he demonstrated expertise in managing teams, ensuring the accuracy of accounting data, attending internal and external audits, performing tax analyses, and creating financial projections. His dedication and proactivity led him to implement the digitization of the office, streamlining processes and reducing manual labor time. Fellippe was recognized for his outstanding performance, receiving the award for outstanding external support satisfaction.

With this solid foundation and a desire for professional growth, Fellippe decided to expand his horizons and embark on a new journey in the United States. He is soon to open the doors of FT FINANCIAL LLC, a financial, audit, and accounting consulting firm based in North Carolina.

In the United States, Fellippe will apply his experience in a more competitive and globalized market, adapting his knowledge to meet the specific needs of American clients. His expertise in financial planning, tax planning, financial analysis, business valuation, cash flow management, debt management advisory, and accounting and bookkeeping makes him a well-rounded professional, equipped to help businesses achieve their financial goals and build a prosperous future.

Fellippe Camara Taborelli da Silva's journey is marked by constant improvement, the pursuit of new challenges, and a passion for contributing to the success of his clients. His experience in different markets and cultures makes him a versatile and capable professional to tackle the challenges of a globalized world.
